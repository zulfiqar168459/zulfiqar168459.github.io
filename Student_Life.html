  Activities:
  • Clubs: There are over 200 clubs and societies covering a wide variety of interests available for you to join or attend.
  • Sports: MEU has 83 University sports clubs, as well as countless college teams and recreational opportunities.
  • Arts: Students share their artistic sense in music, arts and acting through university theater and through campus.


  Trips:

The university offers a variety of travel experinces inside and outside Egypt in which students benefit both socially and accademically.
Furthermore, the academic trips are directly linked to their course work and there's a usually task assigned. The recreational trips are

carefully organized to suit the interests of all students.
